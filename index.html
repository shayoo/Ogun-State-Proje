Vehicular Roof Rack Compliance Implementation Registration and Enforcement Scheme

Welcome to the registration portal for the Vehicular Roof Rack Compliance Implementation Registration and Enforcement Scheme, supported by Ogun State Traffic Compliance and Enforcement (TRACE) Laws.

Project Overview

This initiative aims to establish a Roof Rack/Carrier Permit system for vehicles in Ogun State to enhance road safety and ensure compliance with safety regulations.

Why Register?

Registering your vehicle for a Roof Rack/Carrier Permit helps to:

  • Improve road safety by adhering to safety standards.
  • Protect infrastructure by preventing damage from overloaded vehicles.
  • Support economic growth through compliance and enhanced transport governance.

Get Started

Please fill out the registration form to ensure your vehicle meets the necessary safety standards.
